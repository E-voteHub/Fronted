Welcome to Online Voting

This repository contains the source code for an Online Voting System developed using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js). The system allows users to participate in various types of online voting, such as elections, polls, surveys, and more.

Online voting is a modern way of voting that uses the internet and digital tools to make voting easier and more accessible. It allows people to vote from their phones or computers, which can encourage more people, especially younger voters and those who have trouble getting to polling stations, to take part. It makes the voting process quicker and reduces the need for polling stations and long lines. However, online voting also brings up worries about security and privacy, as well as the risk of hacking. To make it work safely, strong protections are needed. While it offers new possibilities, online voting also has important challenges that need to be carefully addressed.
